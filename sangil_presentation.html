The Sangil People

Maritime Heritage and Cultural Legacy

The Sangil people represent one of the most fascinating ethno-linguistic groups within the Bangsamoro region, with a rich heritage that spans centuries of maritime tradition and Islamic faith.

Primary Language

The Sangil people primarily speak the Sangil/Sangir language, maintaining their linguistic heritage across generations.

A unique secret language developed in the early 20th century, primarily used among Sangirese sailors.
